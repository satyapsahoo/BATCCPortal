Cricket in BC Attaching

To all cricket enthusiasts! Thank you for taking time out to know about us. Lions United a new cricket club formed in 2019 as a cricket department under BC Attaching (established in 1957).

We play in the following leagues:
  • Indoor tournaments in winter
  • Bayern Verbandsliga in summer
  • Outdoor tennis ball tournaments in summer
