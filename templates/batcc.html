Cricket in BC Attaching

To all cricket enthusiasts! Thank you for taking time out to know about us. Lions United a new cricket club formed in 2019 as a cricket department under BC Attaching (established in 1957).

We play in the following leagues:
  • Indoor tournaments in winter
  • Bayern Verbandsliga in summer
  • Outdoor tennis ball tournaments in summer

Our objective is to grow cricket in the region of Munich and Freising. Most of our members are either working in IT and Engineering, and many are students from in and around Munich. We believe in providing equal opportunity to all who seek to enjoy the game of cricket.

As a member of BC Attaching, in addition to cricket, you are also entitled to play football, tennis, baseball, etc in the club. The facilities include a big cricket ground with 70 m boundaries, a spacious indoor hall for winters, clean toilets and changing rooms with showers during the match. You can see the pictures in our facebook page, in the link at the bottom of the page.
